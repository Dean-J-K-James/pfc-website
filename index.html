Phasmophobia Randomiser

To get started, create a challenge code. Either paste an existing one in, or press randomise to make a new one.

Lights

Choose which item should be your primary source of light. All other light sources are banned. Glowsticks are always allowed.

Temperature

Are you allowed to use a thermometer? Disabling makes it harder to find the ghost, but it is still possible determine whether it is freezing or not.

Lockdown

You must stay in the house! When you leave, you cannot re-enter. Collect as much evidence as you can before leaving.

Closets/ Containers

Are you allowed to hide in closets and containers?

Doors

What doors can you interact with? This does not apply to doors that lead to further sections of the map, such as hallways.

Power

Can you turn the power on?

Radio

Are you allowed to open use the radio and global chat features?

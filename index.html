Phasmophobia Randomiser

To get started, create a challenge code. Either paste an existing one in, or press randomise to make a new one.

Lights

Choose which item should be your primary source of light. All other light sources are banned. Glowsticks are always allowed.

Temperature

Are you allowed to use a thermometer? Disabling makes it harder to find the ghost, but it is still possible determine whether it is freezing or not.

Lockdown

Are you allowed to leave and re-enter the house? If no, you must gain all evidence before leaving the building. Checking the camera must come last.
